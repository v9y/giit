##Part 1 (Rafi)##

banatii hai koii na koii kahaanii
ab ke baras dekhe bane kyaa fasaanaa

sach hii kahaa hai pa.nchhii inako
raat ko Thahare to u.D jaae.N din ko
aaj yahaa.N kal vahaa.N hai Thikaanaa
