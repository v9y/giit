jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye
(jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye ke man lalachaaye
giit koii gaaye re sapan mere jiivan ke musakaaye)\-2
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye

koii chhup chhup,
(koii chhup chhup nai.n milaaye, kii paas bulaaye re)\-2
merii kalii kalii khil jaaye, nazar jhuk jaye
ke sun sharamaaye re jhalak diyaa jaaye
ke man moraa pa.nchhii ban u.D jaaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye

(ab chamak chaa.Ndanii raate.n, ho.ngii raate.n
ab dil mil unase baate.n, ho.ngii baate.n)\-2
vo kahe.nge mujhase raanii, ba.Dii maharabaanii,
idhar too aao ta.Dap jiyaa jaaye
kahuu.Ngii tore laaj sharam nahii.n aaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye ke man lalachaaye
giit koii gaaye re sapan mere jiivan ke musakaaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye

mai.ne gin gin,
(mai.ne gin gin baras bitaaye, ke diip jalaaye re)\-2
koii mujhako apane banaaye, ke pyaar jataaye,
rahaa naa ab jaaye kahaa naa jaaye ?
koii merii naino.n kii ni.ndiyaa churaaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye

(ab chamak chaa.Ndanii raate.n, ho.ngii raate.n
ab dil mil unase baate.n, ho.ngii baate.n)\-2
vo kahe.nge mujhase raanii, ba.Dii meharabaanii,
idhar to aao ta.Dap jiyaa jaaye
kahuu.Ngii tore laaj sharam nahii.n aaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye ke man lalachaaye
giit koii gaaye re sapan mere jiivan ke musakaaye
jiyaa lahar lahar laharaaye
