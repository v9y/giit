aanaa hai to aa, raah me.n kuchh pher nahii.n hai
bhagavaan ke ghar der hai, a.ndher nahii.n hai
aanaa hai to aa ...

jab tujhase na sulajhe.n tere ulajhe hue dha.ndhe
bhagavaan ke inasaaf pe sab chho.D de ba.nde
Khud hii terii mushkil ko vo aasaan karegaa
jo tuu nahii.n kar paayaa vo bhagavaan karegaa
aanaa hai to aa ...

kahane kii zaruurat nahii.n, aanaa hii bahut hai
is dar pe teraa siis jhukaanaa hii bahut hai
jo kuchh hai tere dil me.n vo sab usako Khabar hai
ba.nde tere har haal pe maalik kii nazar hai
aanaa hai to aa ...

bin maa.Nge bhii milatii hai.n yahaa.N man kii muraade.n
dil saaf ho jinakaa vo yahaa.N aake sadaa de.n
milataa hai jahaa.N nyaay vo darabaar yahii hai
sa.nsaar kii sab se ba.Dii sarakaar yahii hai
aanaa hai to aa ...
