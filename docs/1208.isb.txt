##Part 1##

mere mahabuub qayaamat hogii
aaj rusavaa terii galiyo.n me.n muhabbat hogii
naam nikalegaa teraa hii lab se
jaan jab is dil-e-naadaan se ruKhasat hogii
mere mahabuub ...

mere sanam ke dar se agar
baad-e-sabaa ho teraa guzar
kahanaa sitamagar kuchh hai Khabar
teraa naam liyaa, jab tak bhii jiyaa
ai shamaa teraa paravaanaa
jisase ab tak tujhe nafarat hogii
aaj rusavaa ...

tere dil ko bhii sanam tujhase shikaayat hogii

terii galii mai.n aataa sanam

Khatm bas aaj ye vahashat hogii
