mai.nne tere liye hii saat ra.ng ke sapane chune
sapane suriile sapane
kuchh ha.Nsate, kuchh Gam ke
terii aa.Nkho.n ke saae churaae
rasiilii yaado.n ne
o mai.nne tere liye ...

chhoTii baate.n
chhoTii-chhoTii baato.n kii hai yaade.n ba.Dii
bhuule nahii.n biitii huii ek chhoTii gha.Dii
janam-janam se aa.Nkhe.n bichhaaii.n
tere liye in raaho.n ne
o mai.nne tere liye ...

bhole-bhaale
bhole-bhaale dil ko bahalaate rahe
tanhaaii me.n tere Khayaalo.n ko sajaate rahe
kabhii-kabhii to aavaaz dekar
mujhako jagaayaa Khvaabo.n ne
o mai.nne tere liye ...

ruuThii raate.n
ruuThii huii raato.n ko manaayaa kabhii
tere liye miiThii subah ko bulaayaa kabhii
tere binaa bhii tere liye hii
diye jalaaye aaho.n ne
o mai.nne tere liye ...
