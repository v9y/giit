jaag dil-e-diivaanaa rut jaagii vasl-e-yaar kii
basii huii zulf me.n aaii hai sabaa pyaar kii 
jaag dil-e-diivaanaa ...

do dil ke kuchh leke payaam aaii hai
chaahat ke kuchh leke salaam aaii hai
dar pe tere subah kha.Dii huii hai diidaar kii
jaag dil-e-diivaanaa ...

ek parii kuchh shaad-sii naashaad-sii
baiThii huii shabanam me.n terii yaad kii
bhiig rahii hogii kahii.n kalii-sii gulazaar kii
jaag dil-e-diivaanaa ...

o mere dil ab Khvaabo.n se mu.Nh mo.D le
biitii huii sab raate.n yahii.n chho.D de
tere to din-raat hai ab aa.Nkho.n me.n diladaar kii
jaag dil-e-diivaanaa ...
